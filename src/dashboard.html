About Us

Cavite State University - Carmona Campus

Individual Multimedia Arts Guild Enthusiasts

iMAGE is a Non-Academic Student Organization at Cavite State University - Carmona Campus.

iMAGE is the creative organization of CVSU Carmona dedicated to producing and archiving visual and multimedia artworks. We are a community of student artists, photographers, writers, and videographers united by a passion for creativity and collaboration. Follow us on our socials and be part of our growing creative community.
